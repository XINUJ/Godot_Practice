The blobfish is quite the sad animal if brought to the surface since when taken away from its natural habitat on the ocean floor, it literally collapses (while dying painfully) on itself due to the changes in pressure, melting into a sad form. I like to imagine one escape from a scientific facility upon somehow gaining immortality and attempt to escape back at sea while fighting monsters (since it is a video game, why not?).
